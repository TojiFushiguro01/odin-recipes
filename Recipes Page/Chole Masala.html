Chole Masala

Chole Masala is a popular Punjabi dish where chickpeas are simmered in a spicy & tangy gravy. "Chole" is a Punjabi word for "Chickpeas". So this dish is nothing but chickpeas cooked to perfection with various spices, onions, tomatoes and herbs. It tastes exactly like the North Indian street-side chole curry, minus the heat. We prefer our foods on the tamer side of the Scoville index, so Iâ€™ve made a milder masala gravy. However, if you prefer spicier food, simply increase the quantity of red chili powder, green chillies and garam masala powder to get the flavor just right for your palate.

Ingridents

  • Dried Amla
  • Dry Pomegranate seeds
  • Salt
  • Tumeric
  • Chole Masala Spices
  • Red, Green chili powder

Steps:

  1. Soak and Cook Chickpeas
  2. Make Chole Masala Powder
  3. Make Masala Gravy
  4. Mix them together and cook on low to medium heat
  5. Eat in Peace!!
